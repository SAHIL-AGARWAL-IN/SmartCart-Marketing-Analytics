About the Project

SmartCart Customer Segmentation

This analytics dashboard processes and segments customer data to define actionable purchase personas. By identifying purchase patterns and demographic traits, the marketing team can implement high-conversion targeted campaigns.

Core Analytics Pipeline

  • Data Preprocessing: Handles missing values in annual incomes by filling with median statistics.
  • Feature Engineering: Computes customer age, duration of membership tenure, total combined spending across 6 categories, total family size, and one-hot encodes education and marital status.
  • Feature Scaling: Normalizes all metrics using z-score standardization to ensure no single variable biases the algorithm.
  • Principal Component Analysis (PCA): Projects the 18-dimensional customer profile space onto 2 principal components to enable visual 2D clustering.
  • K-Means Clustering: Segments customers into 4 distinct groups (K=4) to distinguish purchasing personas (Elite Spenders, Loyal Spenders, Frugal Families, and Budget Shoppers).
